Uber Bosses - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Uber Bosses are special and particularly hard world bosses. Depending upon the zone you find me, I may summon a boss within that zone or provide transportation to another.
In order to perform the summoning I collect currency from all players on this server. Generally once I have finished the summoning I will have to rest for an hour.
