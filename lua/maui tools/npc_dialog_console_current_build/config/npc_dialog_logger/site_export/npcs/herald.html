Tier Currency - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

As you kill mobs throughout the world you will gain various tiers of currency. These currencies can be spent on all kinds of gear, augments, quest items, custom focus, custom spells, and much more.
Currently there exist 8 tiers of currency. If you are new to the server your primary focus will be on the following:
1. [Tier 1 Currency].
2. [Tier 2 Currency].
3. [Tier 3 Currency].
This is the first currency you will encounter on your journey. Each time you kill a mob between levels 1 and 25 you will earn a single token.
As you accumulate these tokens you will want to visit Doug, who can trade your various pieces of gear for them.
Each time you kill a mob between levels 26 and 39 you will earn a single token.
As you accumulate these tokens you will want to visit Flex, who can trade your various pieces of gear for them.
Each time you kill a mob over level 40 you will earn a single token.
As you accumulate these tokens you will want to visit Max, who can trade your various pieces of gear for them.

Ultimate Zones - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Once you have completed your Pre-Ultimate Progression tasks (Talk to Mel), you will gain access to totally custom Ultimate Zones. These zones will be far harder than anything you have encountered, but the rewards will be worth it! You gain Ultimate gear, spells, focii and more as you complete these zones.

Legendary Weapons - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

By consulting with Forge, who resides within the Ascended Hub, you can craft Legendary weapons. These weapons come in various tiers of quality, with higher quality versions being much more potent while also having unique special effects.
In order to increase the quality, often you will need to speak with Weasel, the gambling gnome. He also resides within this zone.

Gambling - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

You will encounter Weasel in various zones, including the Ascended Hub. He can assist you in creating better versions of Legendary weapons, armor, and more. There is always a risk when it comes to gambling, so good luck!

Uber Bosses - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Arch Magus Phil, who resides within this zone as well as other progression zones, can accept certain currencies such as Ultimate Token's.
After he accumulates enough he will spawn an Uber boss for you. Which boss he spawns depends on where he is located. These bosses possess a lot of gear, fun clickies, and booster items...but will require you to bring some friends.
Boogey begins to cast a spell. (Torrent of Divinity)

Ultimate Credits - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Ultimate Credits are an end game currency which can be bought with platinum. With them, you can buy a ton of utility items, potions, appearance items, various services, and much more.
If you want to learn more head over to [Aftershock], as he handles all things Ultimate Credits.

Epic 1.0 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

The short answer is Yes! Fleb, who resides within this zone, can convert your Epic 1.0 into an Epic Augment, which can be faceted into any weapon with the proper augment slot available. You can even do the quest twice to get two!

Pet Weapons - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

John, who is up the ramp a bit within this zone, can provide you with Pet Weapons. As you complete tasks given from Mel, across the way from me, he will offer you better versions of weapons.
