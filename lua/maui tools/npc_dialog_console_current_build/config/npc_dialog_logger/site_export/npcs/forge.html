Ultimate 1.0 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Here is the rundown on Ultimate 1.0 Weapons:
Ultimate Weapons are very special items that provide a considerable boost to your power as well as other benefits, depending upon your class.
I craft these weapons when handed the recipe requirements. All Ultimate 1.0 recipes require (1) Legendary Mold and (1) Ultimate Token from Ultimate Upper & Lower Guk. You may also purchase these with [Ultimate Credits]. Each Ultimate 1.0 will also require a specific vendor item, which can be purchased from Bart, who is across the way from me.
When I craft Ultimate Weapons they will come back in one of four qualities: Normal, Excellent, Superb, and Mastercraft. As you might expect, each quality provides greater stats & damage as well as better procs & click effects.
If you do not get a Mastercraft quality of your Ultimate 1.0 from me, you should consult Weasel. He can help you reroll your weapon through gambling.

How To Craft - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Now that you have proven your worth all you have to do is look through all available Ultimate 1.0's, view the required materials, and then return them to me!

Ultimate 1.5 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Yes! After you have created a Mastercraft quality Ultimate 1.0 you will be able to craft...well...the Ultimate version of it. Let us refer to it as Ultimate 1.5.
You will need to venture into Ultimate Sebilis to obtain materials necessary to craft this.
Also, unlike the Ultimate 1.0, there are not varying qualities associated with the Ultimate 1.5.
Ultimate 1.0 Weapons crafted by me may roll one of the following qualities based on the displayed percent chance:
Excellent: 28%
Mastercraft: 10%
Normal: 40%
Superb: 22%
