Gambling Weapons - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Let's chat about Gambling your Ultimate 1.0:
As Forge most likely told you, when he crafts Ultimate 1.0 weapons, they come in varying qualities. Almost certainly you will want a Mastercraft as you progress.
Much like Forge, when you gamble with me I will return you a random quality associated with the item. In your case here, Normal, Excellent, Superb, and Mastercraft. Your chances of getting a Mastercraft from gambling are higher than when crafting with Forge.
One caveat...As the term suggests, gambling comes with risk. There is a small chance you item will be lost forever when gambling! You can mitigate this, as well as increase your quality chances, by applying [Luck of The Draw].
In this zone gambling only applies to your Ultimate 1.0. Your Ultimate 1.5 will not require any gambling.
Future Ultimate zones will provide you additional opportunities to gamble for better quality gear specific to that zone.

Luck Of The Draw - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Luck of The Draw Potion (Gambling Bonus) grants Luck of The Draw. This can be bought with [Ultimate Credits]. It increases the chances of getting a Mastercraft version of the item you are rolling for by 15 percent. It also will prevent you from losing your item on a bad roll.

How To Gamble - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

First you will want to purchase [Gambling Credits] from me for 5000 platinum.
Alternatively, you can buy Gambling Credits (20) from [Aftershock] and use the item on yourself.
Lastly, just hand me any quality Ultimate 1.0. With a bit of luck, I will return you a better version.
