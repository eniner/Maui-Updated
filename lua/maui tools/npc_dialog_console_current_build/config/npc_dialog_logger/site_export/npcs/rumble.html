Rumble

Zone: Guild Lobby

Overview

Talent Purchase: Assault
Rank 1 (Cost: 20): You charge at your enemy and instantly cast two Savage I attacks. If your target is suffering from Black Plague I and Blistering Malady II, you will issue a third Savage I. 30 second recast.
Additional Detail: You learn an new spell: Assault I.
Additional Detail: Your target must be at least 15 yards away.
Talent Purchase: Empowered Swamp Claw
Ranks 1-20: Up to 100% chance to trigger two additional Swamp Claw I on cast. Increases by 5% per rank.
Ranks 11-20: Up to 60% chance to trigger an additional two more Swamp Claw I on cast. Increases by 3% per rank.
Talent Purchase: Outbreak
Rank 1 (Cost: 5): Black Plague I will now be cast on all enemies within 60 yards.
Talent Purchase: Empowered Savagery
Ranks 1-20: Up to 100% chance to cast Blistering Malady II on all targets when using Savage I. Increases by 2% per rank.
Ranks 11-20: Up to 60% chance to trigger an additional Savagery I on cast. Increases by 2% per rank.
Talent Purchase: Blissful Malaise
Rank 1 (Cost: 5): You learn a new spell: Blissful Malaise I.
Blissful Malaise I: High HP single-target heal.
Talent Purchase: Enraged Minion
Rank 1 (Cost: 5): You pet will receive Enraged Minion I & Resolute Minion I and after casting Seethe I.
Enraged Minion I: Increase pet v2 haste by 30%, Flurry chance by 250% for 30 seconds.
Resolute Minion I: Increase pet regeneration by 750,000 hitpoints per tick.
Talent Purchase: Triassic
Rank 1 (Cost: 5): when casting Seethe I you transform all current minions summoned by Wild Cascade I into ancient beasts, increasing their minimum damage and maximum damage by 500%. 30 second recast.
Talent Purchase: Demigod Boon (BST)
Ranks 1-5: Up to 100% chance to trigger a second Boon of the Summoner II on cast. Increases by 20% per rank.
Talent Purchase: Call of the Wild
Ranks 1-10: Up to 100% chance to trigger an additional servant when casting Wild Cascade I. Increases by 10% per rank.
Ranks 6-10: Up to 60% chance to trigger a second another additional servants when casting Wild Cascade I. Increases by 6% per rank.
Additional Detail: If the caster is on the sorcerer path an additional servant may be summoned.
Talent Purchase: Claw Mastery
Ranks 1-10: Up to 40% chance to trigger three additional melee attacks each time you attack with a hand to hand (fist) weapon. Increases by 4% per rank.
Talent Purchase: Swamp Thing
Rank 1 (Cost: 10): Each time you land a melee attack you have a 20% chance to also attack your target with Savage I (Empowered), which does 50% increased damage over Savage I.
Talent Purchase: Ultimate Pet Fury
Rank 1 (Cost: 10): Your pet will receive additional melee damage & hit points.
