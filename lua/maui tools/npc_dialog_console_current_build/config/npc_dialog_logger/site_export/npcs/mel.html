Pre-Ultimate Progression - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

As a new player your first goal should be to tackle Pre-Ultimate Progression. This progression will require you to acquire & return to me some custom items from familiar raid targets.
The purpose of doing these progression [Tasks] is to help orient you with the basics of this server as well as to gain experience, AA's, and Tier 1 - 3 currency.

Ultimate Rank 1 Access - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

To obtain access to the Ultimate Rank 1 zones, Upper Guk and Lower Guk, you must complete the Crushbone Challenge, which is offered by [Quake]. This challenge is only accessible after completing Pre-Ultimate progression.
1. Before facing Crush the Transformed you must first defeat Dvinn the Tormentor and Ser Darish the Black.
2. Next, defeat Crush the Transformed and loot a Heart of Crush the Transformed. Take this to [Forge] to complete the challenge.
After you have completed the aforementioned prerequisite, simply talk to [Quake] and he will be able to provide you access.

1. Ghoul Lord's Head.
2. Froglok King's Head.
3. Shin Lord's Head.
4. Ancient Croc's Head.
